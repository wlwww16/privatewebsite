Liberty

True "liberty" is the freedom serve God, not to do as you please (which will please the devil). This is not news, but I thought it useful to write down.

16 As free, and not using your liberty for a cloke of maliciousness, but as the servants of God.
— 1 Peter 2:16 KJV

The word "maliciousness" is the Greek κακία, meaning evil, wickedness, depravity. Liberty is not the freedom to fulfill worldly desires and fleshly lusts, as they are evil, wicked, and depraved.
