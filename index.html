FAQ

Why the minimalist design?

I don't like modern web design. It's all corporate, devoid of personality, full of ads and trackers, and generally "bloated". I also find it pleasing to look at a minimalist page, and it's my website. Unlike a Facebook or Twitter page where, at best, you can change a profile icon and small banner, this entire site is my canvas and the design of everything is my personal choice; I could have all text look like this, if I really wanted to.

This is what I want my site to look like. You can make your site look and function how you please, that is the beauty of what the internet should be, without corporate monopolies taking the fun out of it.
